Calculate your goal GPA

To calculate your goal GPA, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Enter the GPA you currently have.
  2. Enter the number of credit hours you have already completed.
  3. Enter the GPA you would like to achieve.
  4. Enter the number of credit hours you plan to take in the upcoming semester(s).
  5. Hit the Calculate button
